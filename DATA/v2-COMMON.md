## How payments work on GIMB V2

**Important: GIMB does NOT handle your money.**

What GIMB does:

* ✅ Track invoices you create
* ✅ Show payment status
* ✅ Send reminders
* ✅ Give you a ledger view

What GIMB does NOT do:

* ❌ Process payments
* ❌ Hold money
* ❌ Guarantee payments

Your money stays between you and your customer.

---

## How credit works on GIMB V2

**Important: GIMB is NOT a lender.**

What GIMB does:

* ✅ Help you build a credit profile
* ✅ Organize your documents
* ✅ Show your trade history (with your permission)
* ✅ Connect you with banks and NBFCs

What GIMB does NOT do:

* ❌ Give loans
* ❌ Make credit decisions
* ❌ Share your data without consent

Think of GIMB as your **preparation partner**, not your lender.
